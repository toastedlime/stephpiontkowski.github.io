Monthly Edit November 2017

Posted on November 10, 2017May 17, 2018 by steph

Welcome to the Monthly Edit. These are things I either already own, want to buy or just dream about.

1 // This rug from McGee + Co is so perfect. I’d put it underneath the outdoor mat below. Bonus: free shipping through the weekend using code TREAT.

2 // I am not a pink person really but for some reason I can’t get these slippers out of my head. I first found them at TJMaxx but of course they didn’t have my size so I found them online at DSW.

3 // I don’t know if I’ll ever own this ottoman but I can certainly dream. This is one of my favorite pieces from Amber Lewis who can seriously do no wrong.

4 // This outdoor doormat from Olive Paper Co would be so gorgeous on top of the McGee + Co rug above. She hand paints every single one! Check out her IG feed and thank me later – it is so pretty!

5 // Splatter tumblers from Chelspots. I bought a planter from her a few years ago at a makers fair and she is so talented. I was somewhere earlier this week and used her Mom so Hard mug and I had to check her out again. She’s local to San Diego which is cool. We have some amazing makers here. It’s so inspiring.

6 // I used to work with Michelle before we all left Florida. She and her hubby moved to Vienna and she really ramped up her photography business. She is a film photographer and her work is just so dreamy and ethereal. Seriously prepare to be blown away. She sells some of her prints through Society 6 so I highly recommend you check it out.

7 // Kassi Grunder is another local maker that I recently found on Instagram and I am super in love with this Grace earring that is the little curve earring that you can wear on one or both sides. I like the one that curves up on the right. She also does some fun craft workshops which hopefully I’ll be able to go to soon!

8 // I have been using Get to Work Book for about a year and I realized that recently I’ve recommended it about a dozen times, no joke. She’s also lives here in San Diego and has designed what I consider to be the best planner I’ve ever used. They’re a little pricey but WORTH IT. The new year will be here before you know it so you should order one of these and be prepare to do the work!

9 // What kind of interior designer would I be if I didn’t give some love to the new Hearth & Hand line at Target. I am so into these plates and I think I need to run and get a set before they’re all gone! The color and shape is exactly what I’ve been looking for.

That’s it for my first installment of Monthly Edit. Let me know in the comments what you think and if you’ve used any of these products.
