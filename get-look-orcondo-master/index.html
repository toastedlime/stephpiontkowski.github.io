Get the Look: Orcondo Master

Posted on August 10, 2018 by steph

One of my favorite bedrooms is the “Orcondo” master designed by Orlando Soria of Hommemaker. It is the absolute perfect mix of masculine and feminine, old and new, with some surprising additions like a handmade quilt from his mom. The whole place is stunning and you can check out the full reveal on Style by Emily Henderson. Today I’m bringing you a Get the Look post for this room. 

chandelier // wall color // quilt // bed // pillows // sconce // nightstand // planter // rug // chair // dresser // lamps // art

This is my take on how to get the look in your own bedroom. What do you think? It’s not a typical combination of pieces but they would be so gorgeous together. Have you used any of these products in your own room? If you have, send me a picture – I’d love to see how you pulled it together.
